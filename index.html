THE WORLD OF STEM.

What is our goal?

Our mission statement is to shed more light towards the STEM fields by allowing teenagers to obtain the opportunity to build a mindset around the topics that heavily surround our generation. We draw info from other resources to collect info about STEM-based programs and workshops and display them for you. These are catered towards highschool students, who are interested in exploring new opportunities in the STEM field.
